What is Boba?

Boba, also known as bubble tea, is a delicious and refreshing drink that originated in Taiwan. It's made with tea, milk, sweeteners, and chewy tapioca pearls that are loved worldwide.

Types of Boba

Classic Milk Tea

Black tea, milk, and chewy tapioca pearls.

Fruit Tea

Refreshing tea with fruity flavors and popping boba.

Matcha Boba

Earthy matcha flavor with creamy milk and pearls.

Taro Milk Tea

Sweet and nutty flavor with a purple hue.

Fun Facts

  • Boba was invented in the 1980s in Taiwan.
  • The tapioca pearls are made from cassava starch.
  • Boba can be served hot or cold!
